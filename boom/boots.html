Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms does it work on?

We support both Android and iPhones of all shapes and sizes. We've explicitly built it to work on extremely low featured Android phones that are popular in Asia

What does "bite-sized" mean?

We live in a distracted, time-poor world. No one has the patience for long, boring content. Backed up strong research, Know is based on bite-sized or micro-content - very short engaging material delivered via 1 - 2 minute podcasts or video clips, engaging images, or even a short paragraph of text. This allows for users to consume this anytime, anywhere

But does this work? What can I do with it?

Micro-content has been proven extremely effective, particularly for targetted interventions. If you want your employees to remember, engage and act, short form content is perfect. Sure, we can't probably teach quantum physics through it, but then again how many of us are trying to do that?
